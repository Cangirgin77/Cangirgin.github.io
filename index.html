Projects

Multigeneration Geothermal Power Plant

This senior design project focuses on developing a multigeneration system that uses geothermal energy to simultaneously produce electricity, hydrogen, and freshwater. The system integrates Rankine Cycle, PEM electrolyzer, and multistage desalination to demonstrate sustainable and efficient energy generation.

ANAFARTA KTR 2025 – Autonomous Combat UAV

Developed for the TUBITAK 2025 competition, ANAFARTA is a fully autonomous UAV optimized for surveillance and combat missions. It includes real-time image processing, target tracking, aerodynamic optimization, and energy-efficient operation strategies.

Renewable Energy + Hydrogen Storage

This term project proposes a hybrid renewable energy system integrating solar and wind with hydrogen storage. The system ensures stable energy supply for university campuses and evaluates technical and economic feasibility using demand models and ROI/LCOE analysis.

Autonomous Rudder Steering Systems for Boats (Ongoing)

This project focuses on developing an autonomous rudder control system for boats to improve stability and route precision. It is currently under development and will include embedded feedback mechanisms and control algorithms.
